    Product Details

    A Punjabi T-shirt is a vibrant garment that reflects the rich cultural heritage of the Punjabi community. Typically made of comfortable cotton fabric, it features intricate embroidery, often showcasing traditional motifs such as phulkari or colorful patterns inspired by Punjabi folklore. The T-shirt is adorned with lively colors, representing the lively spirit and joyous celebrations synonymous with Punjabi culture. These garments often incorporate elements like the iconic turban or symbols of agricultural prosperity. Wearing a Punjabi T-shirt is not just a fashion choice; it's a proud display of cultural identity, embodying the warmth, exuberance, and tradition of the Punjabi lifestyle.
